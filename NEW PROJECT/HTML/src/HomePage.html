OUR HISTORY

Our organization has a rich and varied heritage, started in 1948 our Founding Director, Maxton Strong was given a section of land in the middle of the jungles of North India by the newly established Indian Government. There in the jungle, among wild animals, disease and struggle our organization was born. Over the years as our family and facility grew, so too did the local area; We continue to care, educate, entertain and support our children to the very best of our abilities.
